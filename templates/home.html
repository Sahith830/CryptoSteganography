Encryption & Decryption

Steganography is the practice of concealing a file, message, image, or video within another file, message, image, or video. In image steganography, a message is embedded into an image by altering the values of some pixels, which are chosen by an encryption algorithm. The recipient of the image must be aware of the same algorithm in order to know which pixels he or she must select to extract the message.
